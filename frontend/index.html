Anime is famous all over the world, and over the years, I have watched many incredible series, but some have left a lasting impact on me. Today, I am going to summarize my all-time top six anime series that I loved.

Latest Pokemons

1. Attack On Titan

IMDb rating : 9.1/10
2013-2023
Awards - 40 awards & 88 nominations total

In a world overrun by man-eating Titans, humanity fights for survival behind massive walls. Eren Yeager, driven by vengeance, joins the elite Survey Corps to uncover the truth about these monstrous beings. As dark secrets unfold, the battle for freedom becomes more than just survival. Betrayal, sacrifice, and shocking revelations reshape the fate of mankind.

2. One Piece

IMDb rating : 9.0/10
1999-still continuing
Awards - 6 awards & 13 nominations total

Monkey D. Luffy dreams of becoming the King of the Pirates and sets sail in search of the legendary One Piece. With his quirky crew, he faces ruthless enemies, explores mysterious islands, and unlocks hidden powers. As the seas become deadlier, friendships and dreams are tested. The journey for freedom, adventure, and treasure never ends.

3. Naruto: Shippuden

IMDb rating : 8.7/10
2007-2017
Awards - 2 awards & 10 nominations total

Naruto Uzumaki, a mischievous outcast, dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and earning his village’s respect. Through hard work, friendships, and intense battles, he uncovers his true destiny. Facing powerful foes and inner demons, he must protect his loved ones at all costs. The path to becoming Hokage is filled with pain, growth, and legendary showdowns.

4. Death Note

IMDb rating : 8.9/10
2006-2007
Awards - 2 wins & 1 nomination total

A high school genius, Light Yagami, stumbles upon a mysterious notebook that grants him the power to kill anyone by writing their name. As he envisions a world without crime, a cat-and-mouse game with the brilliant detective L begins. Morality blurs as justice and tyranny collide. In this psychological thriller, one wrong move could mean death.

5. Dragon Ball Z

IMDb rating : 8.8/10
1996-2003
Top rated TV #67

Goku, a Saiyan warrior with an insatiable thirst for battle, embarks on epic adventures to become the strongest fighter. Alongside friends and rivals, he faces powerful foes, cosmic threats, and earth-shaking tournaments. With each battle, he breaks his limits and unlocks new transformations. The fate of the universe often hangs on his strength and willpower.

6. Pokemon

IMDb rating : 7.6/10
1997-2023
Awards - 1 nomination

Ash Ketchum sets out on a journey to become a Pokémon Master, traveling through different regions with his loyal Pikachu. Along the way, he befriends trainers, captures powerful Pokémon, and battles formidable Gym Leaders. Team Rocket lurks in the shadows, always scheming. With adventure, friendship, and endless battles, the Pokémon world is full of excitement!
